Hinton, WV Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Hinton is a city in Summers County, West Virginia, United States. The population was 2,266 at the 2020 census. It is the county seat of Summers County. Hinton was established in 1873 and chartered in 1897. Hinton was named after John 'Jack' Hinton, the original owner of the town site.
