Buckhead Ridge, FL Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Buckhead Ridge is a census-designated place (CDP) in Glades County, Florida, United States. The population was 1,450 at the 2010 census. The community consists of a few small commercial developments and a large mobile home park. Most residential lots in the community abut canals that feed into Lake Okeechobee.
