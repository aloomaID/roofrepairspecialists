Pelican Marsh is a census-designated place (CDP) in northwestern Collier County, Florida, United States. It is bordered to the west by Naples Park and to the south by Pine Ridge, and it is 8 miles (13 km) north of Naples. U.S. Route 41 (Tamiami Trail) forms the western edge of the community.
