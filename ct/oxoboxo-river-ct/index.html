Oxoboxo River, CT Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Oxoboxo River /ˈɑːksoʊˌbɑːksoʊ/ is a census-designated place (CDP) in the town of Montville in New London County, Connecticut, United States. The population was 2,938 at the 2000 census and 3,165 at the 2010 census. The statistical area encompasses the town center of Montville, as well as the adjacent villages of Palmertown and Uncasville.
